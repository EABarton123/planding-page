About me...


My name is Kim, I am a mother to a little girl and live in North West England. Since having my little girl I have come to realise how unsustainable, ill fitting and limited baby and childrens clothing wear can be. I started just making a couple of pieces for my daughter, with locally sourced materials I managed to lower my carbon footprint for each outfit she wore. A few friends started asking for some of the pieces and then the business was evolved. I still have the same ethos today as I did when I was only making for my daughter.
